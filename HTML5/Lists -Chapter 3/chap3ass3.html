Muffin Recipe

A basic recipe. You can add pretty much anything to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2200g caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegitable oil
  • 400g plain flour
  • 3tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to gas mark 6 or a medium temperature. Line muffin tray with paper muffin cases.
  2. Beat the eggs in a mixing bowl and then mix in the oil and milk. Add the sugar and whisk untill dissolve.
  3. Sift in the dry ingrediants;
  4. Fill muffin cases TWO THREE full
  5. leave to cool(or enjoy warm)!
